Remarks

The core Refine(AssessmentResult, IReadOnlyList<MigrationOutcome>) is pure and deterministic. Candidate configurations are ranked by the Wilson lower bound of their observed satisfaction rate so that small samples cannot outrank larger, consistently-satisfactory ones, and ties deterministically favor the baseline to avoid unnecessary churn.
